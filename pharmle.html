How to Play

1

Read the patient case — age, condition, and clinical presentation are all clues to the drug being used.

2

Select your drug from the dropdown and press Dispense. You have 6 attempts to identify the correct medication.

3

After each wrong guess, a new pharmacology clue is revealed — mechanism, side effects, interactions, or lab findings.

4

The tile grid compares your guess across 8 dimensions: Drug Class, Sub-class, Mechanism, Indication, Route, Monitoring, Side Effect Profile, and Controlled Status.

■ Green = exact  ■ Amber = close  ■ Blue = same family  ■ Red = wrong

A new case drops every day at midnight UTC.

About Pharmle

Pharmle is a daily drug identification puzzle for pharmacy students, pharmacists, and healthcare professionals. Each day presents a real-world patient case — your job is to identify the medication from clinical and pharmacological clues.

Cases cover common drug classes seen in practice — from cardiovascular and endocrine to psychiatry and infectious disease. Difficulty is calibrated for pharmacy students and techs, with enough nuance to challenge working pharmacists.

New case every day at midnight UTC.
